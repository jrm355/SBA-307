The Pup Who Knew too Much


COMING SOON

Summary

Mittens had it all, a loving family, a nice yard to run around in, constant dog park visits, but it all changed when her family adopted a new dog Noodles. Suddenly, all their attention went to Noodles, and her gourmet hand made snacks were suddenly store bought. But she never would have thought, that her snacks were actually going to Noodles. Armed with this new information Mittens does all she can, to reclaim her snacks.
